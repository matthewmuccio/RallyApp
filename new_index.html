About Rally

While the application for Rally in the context of a hackathon is apparent, there are a variety of other uses for the platform. If there is an event, formal or informal, where people going need to collaborate and split into teams, Rally is the platform to use. From creating specialized study groups in a course, to putting together teams for a pick-up basketball game, to finding the right people with the right skills for a hackathon, everyone can Rally.
